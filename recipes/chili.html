Chili

Description

This turkey chili will make you forget all about beef! It's easy and cheap to make, and can be put together in an instant pot

Don't let the large list of ingredients scare you. You'll master this in no time.

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey
  • Chicken OR beef broth
  • Diced canned tomatoes
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Cook the ground turkey till it's just about fully cooked.
  2. Combine all the ingedients together in the instant pot.
  3. Mix together and set the pot to 375 for 15 min.
  4. Let naturally release.
  5. Let cool off and enjoy!
