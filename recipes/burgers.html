Burgers

Description

These burgers can be cooked in the oven, stovetop, or grill. Lots of options.

Become a burger master by following the instructions below, and don't be afraid to experiment!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Variouis spices
  • American cheese
  • Your favorite buns

Directions

  1. Form the ground beef into balls about 1 inch round.
  2. Smash them into patties.
  3. Spice those bad boys up!
  4. Throw them into the oven (375) for 30 minutes. The stove for 5 min each side on medium high heat, or grill!
