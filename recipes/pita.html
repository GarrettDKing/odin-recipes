Pita

Description

Do you like warm bread? How about the satisfaction that comes when making it? This is for you!

You'll love the smell as this one comes together..

Ingredients

  • Flour - can be normal or whole wheat
  • Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Add a half cup of flour and warm water to a teaspoon of the yeast
  2. Once it has sat for 15 min, add the rest of the flour
  3. Mix together then form into seperated 2-inch balls after two hours
  4. Put on stovetop for no more than 90 seconds a side.
